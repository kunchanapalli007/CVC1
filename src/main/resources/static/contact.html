About Cincinnati Volleyball Club

Cincinnati Volleyball Club (CVC) is a passionate community of volleyball enthusiasts located in the heart of Ohio. Established to promote competitive and recreational volleyball, CVC offers a platform for players of all skill levels to connect, grow, and compete.

Our club organizes seasonal tournaments, weekend matches, training sessions, and community events designed to encourage team spirit, athletic development, and healthy living. Whether you're a seasoned player or just starting out, CVC is your home to spike, set, and soar!
